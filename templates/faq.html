Frequently Asked Questions
1. Do I have to pay to participate in the programs? ▼
We aim to keep De Kwekerij as accessible as possible. So you can enter all of our flourishing classes for free. We ask for a donation afterwards. If you really like it you can become a member for €7 a moth. Some of our events include a dinner and have a small fee. If money is a barrier, just let us know — we'll always look for a solution.

4. Can I volunteer or collaborate with De Kwekerij? ▼
Absolutely! De Kwekerij thrives thanks to people who want to share their time, talents, or ideas. Whether you're a natural host, have a great concept for a new session, or want to partner up in your city — drop us a line. We'd love to meet you over a cup of coffee or tea.

6. Are the programs in Dutch or English? ▼
7. What makes De Kwekerij different from other personal development programs? ▼
At De Kwekerij we believe personal growth comes by exchanging knowledge and sharing experiences with others. We are community driven which means we develop people by discovering their passions and talents and experiment in safe situations. No pressure to perform, but real conversations, honest connection, and space to breathe.
8. Is it common for people to attend events on their own? ▼
Definitely. In fact, many people come solo — and leave with new conversations, insights, or even friendships. You don't have to put on a show — just being yourself is more than enough.
