WELCOME TO THE  MENTORING PROGRAM

What's a Mentoring Program?

In a world that is constantly changing, meaningful mentoring is most welcome. De Kwekerij's mentoring programme brings together experienced professionals and committed young people to learn and grow. This programme is specially designed for people who find social commitment and meaning important in their (working) lives and for mentors who want to guide others on their (career) path from their own experience.

During a 1-on-1 session, mentor and mentee walk together for a while. The conversations take place in an equal setting. So sometimes a mentor also learns something from a mentee. Mentors offer a listening ear and allow you to think more deeply about your own development through personal attention. On themes like making choices, relationships and moral ambition, they bring in their own experience and sometimes network.

We make matches based on common interests. And there is always an introductory meeting first, after which you may or may not continue.

The cost of making a match from De Kwekerij is €200, but for the first 10 matches this year we offer a 50% discount. Otherwise, all interviews are free of charge.
