Intergenerational Dinner

Join us at the table for an evening of good vegetarian food and meaningful conversations between younger and older adults.

What is Wisdom?

Tickets: €21.- including 3-course dinner, drinks and a well-organized program.
During the Intergenerational Dinner, we step out of our generational bubbles to exchange wisdom, because life's big questions don't just concern young adults, but older generations too.

Over a shared meal, we challenge stereotypes and create a safe space to share stories, experiences, and life lessons.
Are you in? Do you know an older adult that may be interested? Spread the word!
