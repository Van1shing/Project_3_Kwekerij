Join Our Community

Are you an international student? A newcomer to Amsterdam? You're welcome here.
At De Kwekerij, we believe in the power of community. Our mission is to create a welcoming space for everyone, regardless of where you come from. Whether you're an international student, a recent arrival in Amsterdam, or a local resident, we invite you to be part of our vibrant and inclusive community. Discover friendship, creativity, and a sense of belonging here.

For Whom

  • Young Adults
    Explore new horizons and build lifetime connections. Young adults from all walks of life find a supportive network at De Kwekerij, where personal growth and shared experiences are at the forefront.
  • Refugees
    Find a safe haven and community support at De Kwekerij. We are committed to providing a friendly and resourceful environment where refugees can connect, learn, and thrive together.
  • Locals
    Join a community that values diversity and local pride. Our local members bring a wealth of knowledge and a warm heart, ensuring everyone feels at home in Amsterdam.
