What can you expect?

We offer our training courses in-company or at one of our locations (in the heart of Amsterdam). Of course, we also provide custom work. So if you have a specific question, for example about breaking patterns or having good conversations, let us know! We are happy to put together a custom training course in consultation or give one of our regular workshops.

About De Kwekerij

De Kwekerij provides training and inspiration sessions specialized in the bigger questions and challenges of professionals. What is my calling? How am I meaningful? How do I let go of old certainties and make room for new development? But also how do I deal with conflict and (old school) stress? We know the world of millennials and generation Z like no other. This also applies to the challenges that this generation and their managers face. We are happy to share our knowledge and expertise!

Interested in a training or lecture at your workplace? Please contact us. We would be happy to tell you more about the possibilities.
