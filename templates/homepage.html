Grow together at De Kwekerij

De Kwekerij is a community initiative based in Amsterdam that brings internationals and locals together through activities focused on connection, reflection, and personal growth. We organize monthly workshops for young adults on themes like identity, relationships, and mental health, language cafés for newcomers to practice Dutch in an informal setting, and intergenerational dinners where young people and seniors exchange wisdom and stories over a shared meal. Whether you're here to learn, connect, reflect, or just meet new people, you're welcome. Our sessions are interactive, low-pressure, and designed to help you feel part of a vibrant, supportive community—no matter where you're from or how long you've been in the city.
