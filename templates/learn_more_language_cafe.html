Language Cafe

Join us while learning Dutch language in a relaxed and informal setting.

Dutch language lessons for newcomers

Every Tue 18 · 19:30–21:00
At the Language Café, we chat about all kinds of topics — from talents and hobbies to family and everyday life. We also include seasonal themes that fit the time of year, and regularly explore Dutch culture through books, art, and customs.

Now and then, we do something fun together, like going bouldering or canoeing. You can also get matched with a language buddy to practice even more.
